About FRSS

FRSS is an intelligent flood risk monitoring and reporting platform designed to enhance disaster preparedness across Indian cities using real-time weather data and AI-driven analysis.

Why Choose FloodML?

By combining live weather APIs, machine learning models, and location-aware scoring, FRSS delivers city-specific flood risk predictions, interactive dashboards, and AI-generated summaries — all built to help communities anticipate, assess, and act on flood threats more effectively.
